Times Tables App - Times Tables Quest

A Times Tables App for kids on mobile designed to help your child learn the times tables using multiplication and division questions. The game Times Tables Quest is available for download on your iPhone, iPad, Android phone or tablet.

A learning experience like no other, this game lets kids play their way through the times tables!

With nine characters to choose from, select a hero in this retro pixel platform based adventure. [read more...]

There are 24 hand crafted levels to complete while answering multiplication questions. For each correct answer the hero gets a tick, but for a wrong answer, a tick is removed.

In each level there are hidden areas with coins and gems to collect, but also twelve mysterious gifts to pick up. These gifts can make the level wobble or even turn it upside down! Whatever happens avoid falling in the water or the level restarts.

As the hero progresses, then more awards are given, with a maximum of thirteen to collect, each award adds to your overall score.

Even when all levels have been completed, the hero can still continue to play random levels or just focus on specific times tables.

Internationally pupils learn up to the ten times tables, but in Time Tables Quest this can be changed to the twelve times tables for UK based players.

This game was developed by a parent, their child and a math teacher. With children in mind, no data is collected by the app, there are no in-app purchases, no device permissions. There isn’t a leaderboard, no peer pressure.

If you play it and like it - please review it, so others will know about the game!

Top tips

Look for cracks and sign posts, these will show you where there are hidden areas.
Collect all the coins and jems to have a chance of getting maximum points.
Any remaining time is added to points.
Answer the questions correctly to get top marks.
Complete the level before time runs out to increase your rating. Your score is multiplied by the rating.
The more awards you get, the more points are added to your score.
You are able to change the times tables from 10 to 12 in the settings. This will reset your game.
There is a practice setting where you can set the times tables you would like ot focus on.
For casual gaming, you can select to play a random level too.
The overal game score is the level points added to the award points.
Beware! Not all the pick up gifts are helpful.
